A 1st Hour Project by: Kry'niyah Brown

Movies
  • Friday:follows two friends, Craig and Smokey, over the course of one day in South Central Los Angeles
  • DreamGirls:The story follows their rise to fame, driven by an ambitious manager who demotes the original lead singer, Effie White, and replaces her with Deena Jones, leading to professional and personal turmoil for the group.
  • Don't Say A Word: psychiatrist Nathan Conrad must coax a secret, six-digit number from a traumatized and non-verbal patient, Elisabeth, to save his kidnapped daughter.
  • Poetic Justice: a young poet, Justice, who copes with grief from her boyfriend's murder by writing poetry.
  • The Maze Runner: The Maze Runner is about a group of teenage boys, called Gladers, who are trapped in a large, enclosed area called the Glade, with no memory of how they got there.
  • The Hangover: three groomsmen who lose the groom, Doug, during a wild and memorable bachelor party in Las Vegas.
  • 27 Dresses: a romantic comedy about Jane Nichols, a perpetual bridesmaid and people-pleaser who has been a bridesmaid 27 times. Her life becomes complicated when her younger sister, Tess, falls for Jane's boss.
  • Letter to Juilet: an aspiring writer named Sophie visits Verona, Italy, where she discovers a lost letter written 50 years prior to Juliet Capulet. She responds to the letter, and its author, an elderly woman named Claire, is inspired to return to Italy to find her long-lost love.

  • The Orginals:a supernatural drama series about the Mikaelson family, the first vampires, as they return to New Orleans to reclaim their city
